## 🇬🇧 English Version

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Mechanical Design, Manufacturing, and Automation at Chongqing University (Rank: 1/97). I am deeply passionate about **Robotics System Modeling & Control**, **Intelligent Decision-Making**, and **Autonomous Perception**. I am currently looking for a Ph.D. position in related fields. 

My research bridges the gap between hardware dynamics and advanced algorithms, from decoupled control of asymmetric vector propulsion to high-dimensional optimization in multi-agent systems.

### 🔬 Research & Projects
* **🌊 6-DOF Underwater Grasping Robot:** Designed a novel 4-thruster + 2-servo asymmetric vector propulsion configuration. Developed low-level kinematic decoupling and visual servoing control systems using C++ and ROS2, augmented by a GAN-based underwater image enhancement framework.
* **🎯 Weapon-Target Assignment (WTA) Optimization:** Proposed a cascade failure model and a topology-adaptive truncation strategy for dense target scenarios. Solved the MINLP problem using a modified GS-MOPSO algorithm. Conducted multi-UAV swarm simulations via XTDrone and ROS.
* **🏎️ Autonomous Smart Car System:** Developed visual recognition and high-speed motion control algorithms (C++) for an Edgeboard (Ubuntu) platform, significantly improving line-tracking robustness under extreme conditions *(First Prize, National University Smart Car Competition)*.
